About us

THE COMMONWEALTH OF ZION ASSEMBLY

Birthed over Twenty-Two years ago in the city of Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria, The Commonwealth of Zion Assembly has touched countless lives across the nations of the world. COZA has grown by leaps and bounds with a combined membership of tens of thousands in all her Campuses and eChurch globally. COZA cuts across people from diverse backgrounds, multiple ethnicities and professions. The Senior Pastors, Rev. Biodun and Pastor Modele Fatoyinbo,are seasoned ministers of the gospel with a heart for God’s people and a passion for national transformation.

Our Mission

To raise a TAKEOVER GENERATION who will take over in every sphere, system and sector, not taking sides but spear-heading new frontiers in spirituality and excellence while spreading abroad through prosperity.

Our Vision

To make disciples of all nations, reaching the unchurched who will re-establish God’s kingdom on earth and to inform the nations adequately.

THE TENENT OF COZA

The Trinity

There is only one God who exists in three distinct persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

God`s Word

The Bible is God’s word to us to lead us to Himself and to guide our lives. It is without error and is the authority on which we base our faith, conduct and teachings.

The Saviour

Jesus Christ is the Son of God who came to this earth as Saviour of the world.

The Holy Spirit

Through His Holy Spirit, God lives in and through us now

God Creation

Nothing in creation “just happened”. God created all things by His word and sustains all things and He is the final authority in every situation.

Salvation in Christ

Jesus died on the cross, shed His blood for our sins and rose again on the third day. Salvation is found by placing our faith in what Jesus did for us on the cross.

The Packaging of Salvation

Through His Holy Spirit, God lives in and through us now

We are Overcomers

As children of God, we are overcomers, and more than conquerors and God intends for each of us to experience the abundant life He has in store for us.

Baptism of The Holyspirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit evidenced by the speaking of other tongues.

Baptism By Water

We believe in the baptism of water by immersion signifying the death, burial and resurrection of our Lord Jesus.

The Efficacy Of Prayers

We believe in the power and efficacy of prayer

The Holy Communioun

We believe in the Holy Communion signifying the body and blood of our Lord Jesus Christ

The Ministry of Angels

We believe in the ministry of angels as servants of the heirs of salvation

The Second Coming

We believe in the ministry of angels as servants of the heirs of salvation

Heaven And Earth

Heaven and hell are real places. Death is a beginning, not the end.
